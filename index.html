Most breaches start with people.

We make people Wicked Secure.

Let's talk about what we can do for you.

Wicked Secure provides security awareness consulting focused on the actions and culture of your organization.

As computers and networks get more secure, attackers are focusing their efforts on people. Ninety percent of breaches involve people making a bad choice, from clicking a link to reusing a password. Wicked Secure will work with your security team to identify risky behaviors and replace them with secure ones. People can be the biggest risk, but with the right tools they can become your strongest security control.

Services

Knowing is ONLY half the battle.

Security awareness is more than just what your people know. It is what they do. Wicked Secure replaces bad security behaviors with good ones. When your people make better choices, your organization will be more secure.

Hanging up a poster doesn’t change a culture. Wicked Secure has proven methods to build a culture of security from orientation to operations. Changing your social norms about security makes risky behaviors uncool.
